Phoenix Tri Aquathlon Series

A multi-race aquathlon series for children and adults, covering varying distances at The Guildford Lido and Stoke Park

Races for All Ages & Abilities

The Phoenix Aquathlon Series is made up of multiple races for Tristar age groups (age 8-14), a race for youth/juniors and one for adults (age 15+) on a flat and fast circuit at The Guildford Lido.

The swim takes place in the open air, 50m lido pool and the run is around the traffic-free perimeter of Stoke Park. We welcome entrants of all abilities!

Guildford Lido

Guildford Lido is one of the few remaining 1930s outdoor pools set in four acres of beautifully landscaped gardens. Heated to 24°C in summer months, the Olympic-sized pool is a fantastic venue for our aquathlon series.

Adult Swim

The adult swim starts after the kids races with a staggered start so that everyone gets a time. The transition area is at the opposite end of the lido from the kids transition area.

Child Swim

The kids swim starts at one end of the pool with a timer and an assistant. Kids exit straight to a dedicated transition area (no adults permitted) ready to don their trainers and start the run.

Run Routes

Adults: Start from the main lido car park, past student halls, Guildford Bowling Club, through Stoke Park alongside the Parkway to the roundabout, then circling back. Fully marshalled tarmac footpaths.

Children: Exit the lido area near registration, turn right along the Parkway with marshals at each turnaround point.

We Support All Our Athletes All The Way

Phoenix Triathlon is a friendly club based in Guildford and the wider area. We always welcome new members of all abilities with a healthy 50/50 mix of male and female members of all ages 18+.

Come along to a free coached taster session (Track, Swim or Spin) to see if Phoenix is the right club for you. It's completely free with no obligation to join.

Incredible Value for Money

As a full club member, you can attend over 300 coached sessions a year using great facilities at no extra cost. Join us at regular social events and enjoy discounts with club suppliers and partners.

We'll give you all the help, support and motivation you need to achieve your triathlon or multi-sport goals.

Meet New Training Buddies

The club organises training sessions throughout the week in all disciplines. All standards are welcome with experienced athletes providing help and advice.

Outdoor training depends on weather and time of year, with the die-hards out in all conditions!
